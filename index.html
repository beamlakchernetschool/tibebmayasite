About Tibebmaya

Supportive, modern tutoring customized to each learner.

Tibebmaya was founded to make high-quality tutoring accessible and friendly. Our approach mixes hands-on activities, diagnostic assessments, and targeted practice. Tutors provide clear learning goals and frequent progress reports so families always know the next steps.
